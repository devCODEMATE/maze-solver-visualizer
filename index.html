Maze Solver Visualizer

A simple front-end project inspired by AI search algorithms.

How to use

  • Click cells to add or remove obstacles.
  • The green cell is the start point.
  • The red cell is the goal point.
  • Select BFS or DFS and click "Solve Maze".
